About
Good Design(GD) Korea Awards

Eligibility:

Companies, Government-affiliated Organizations

Products that have been on sale or are scheduled for release, either in
Korea or internationally, within the two years prior to the application date.

Good Design (GD) Korea is a prestigious design award program with a
history of over 40 years. It recognizes products with outstanding design by
granting them the official GD Mark certified by the Korean government.
